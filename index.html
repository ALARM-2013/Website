About Alarm Robotics

Alarm Robotics is team number 2079 in the FIRST Robotics Competition. The team is affiliated with Millis High School in Millis, Massachussets. It was founded in 2006 by Mr. Matt Haggerty and his son Andrew Haggerty, and the team spent their first years together in the Haggerty's garage. As of right now, the team is renting space in Franklin, Massachussets

About FIRST: FIRST website

FIRST, or For Inspiration of Science and Technology, is an organization co-founded by Dr. Dean Kamen and Dr. Woody Flowers in order to inspire kids to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering and mathematics. The competition kicks off on the first Saturday in January, and the teams involved then have 6 weeks to build a robot to compete in the given game.

This years game: Rebound Rumble


This years game, Rebound Rumble, is a game similar to basketball. Robots in alliances need to gather up to three basketballs at a time and shoot them into hoops at three different heights. In the center of the field, there is a bump to stop the robots from crossing over with a ramp that can be lowered to allow the robot to cross over.
